Corporate Services

Blackmore Corporate Solutions can support your company through the bid development process and manage responding to RFPs from start to finish. Our writers and project management professionals take the stress out of crafting targeted and comprehensive RFP responses that clearly communicate your organization's value over other bidders. We also write and evaluate proposals used by grant-seekers and in sales presentations.

Proposal/RFP Services

  • Reviewing current RFP response process and providing potential areas for improvement.
  • Turnkey RFP Project Management including schedule management for all stakeholders and completing the Proposal to meet RFP deadlines.
  • Response compilation as well as ensuring documentation from all contributors, including internal stakeholders, meets RFP guidelines and answers all questions.

Branding

  • Creating attractive and organized design templates for RFP responses, internal communications and other applications.
  • Reviewing existing products, websites and social media for brand cohesiveness.
  • Writing text, creating graphics and editing photos to best showcase your company image.

Document Management

  • Reviewing and formatting corporate documents including Consulting Reports (Geo, QAES, Environmental, etc.), HSE Manuals, JHAs, SOPs, Execution Plans and Quality Programs.
  • Compiling required manuals, plans and documents for RFPs or certifications.
  • Conducting research as necessary with clients, internal stakeholders and external organizations to produce required materials.
